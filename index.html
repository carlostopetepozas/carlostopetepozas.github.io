Carlos Topete Pozas

Remote Sensing · Spatial Data Science · Urban & Ecosystem Dynamics

I develop spatial analytical frameworks to quantify land-cover change, disturbance impacts, and urban–environmental dynamics across scales, with research interests spanning landscape ecology, urban planning, and regional systems. My work integrates multi-sensor satellite time series (e.g., Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS), landscape metrics, and statistical modeling within a broader spatial data science framework. I focus on cross-scale processes that shape rural–urban transitions, landscape gradients, and nature–city interactions.
