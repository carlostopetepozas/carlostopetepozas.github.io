Carlos Topete Pozas

Remote Sensing · Spatial Data Science · Urban & Ecosystem Dynamics

I develop spatial analytical frameworks to quantify land-cover change, disturbance impacts, and urban–environmental dynamics across scales. My work integrates multi-sensor satellite time series (Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS), landscape metrics, and statistical modeling within a spatial data science framework. I focus on cross-scale processes shaping rural–urban transitions, landscape gradients, and nature–city interactions.
